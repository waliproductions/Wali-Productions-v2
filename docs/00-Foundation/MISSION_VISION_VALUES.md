# Purpose

This document defines the mission, vision, and core values of Wali Productions LLC.

---

# Mission

The highest purpose of Wali Productions LLC is to glorify Jesus Christ through every aspect of the business.

Wali Productions LLC provides technology and digital solutions that serve people, strengthen organizations, solve real problems, and reflect biblical integrity, excellence, stewardship, and service.

---

# Vision

To build a respected Christian veteran-owned technology company known for technical excellence, trustworthy service, faithful principles, and meaningful impact.

---

# Core Values

## Christ First

Jesus Christ is the foundation of Wali Productions LLC.

The company exists to honor Him before profit, popularity, opportunity, or growth.

## Integrity

Wali Productions LLC seeks to operate with honesty, transparency, and dependability.

Clients should be able to trust both the work and the word of the company.

## Excellence

The company pursues high-quality work because excellence reflects discipline, care, and service.

The goal is not merely to complete projects, but to complete them well.

## Service

Wali Productions LLC uses technology to serve people and organizations.

Every project should help a client move forward, solve a problem, communicate better, operate more efficiently, or accomplish a meaningful mission.

## Stewardship

Skills, knowledge, tools, time, and opportunities are responsibilities entrusted to the company.

They should be used wisely and faithfully.

## Discipline

As a veteran-owned company, Wali Productions LLC values discipline, responsibility, structure, and follow-through.

## Purpose

Technology should not be used aimlessly.

Every solution should have a clear reason, a practical function, and a meaningful outcome.

---

# Business Conduct

Wali Productions LLC should conduct business in a manner consistent with its Christian foundation.

This includes:

- Keeping commitments
- Speaking truthfully
- Avoiding deceptive practices
- Treating clients with respect
- Providing honest recommendations
- Refusing work that conflicts with the company's convictions
- Delivering quality work to the best of the company's ability

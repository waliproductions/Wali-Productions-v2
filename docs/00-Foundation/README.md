# Purpose

Welcome to the official documentation for the Wali Productions LLC Digital Headquarters project.

This documentation exists to provide a single, authoritative source of information about the company, its mission, governance, business operations, technical architecture, branding, and website.

All project work should begin by reading this document and then `PROJECT_TRUTH.md`.

---

# Documentation Hierarchy

Documents are organized by authority.

## 1. Founder

The Founder of Wali Productions LLC has final authority over all decisions.

Current instructions from the Founder supersede all documentation.

## 2. Canonical Documents

Canonical documents contain approved information representing the official position of Wali Productions LLC.

These documents may only be changed through deliberate review and explicit approval by the Founder.

## 3. Engineering Documents

Engineering documents describe how approved requirements are implemented.

They may recommend improvements but shall not redefine company identity or business direction.

## 4. Reference Documents

Reference documents provide supporting guidance, planning, or historical information.

They are informative rather than authoritative.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence systems are advisory and implementation tools.

AI may generate code, produce documentation, recommend improvements, review implementations, and assist with planning.

AI may not determine company mission, statement of faith, business strategy, branding, governance, or official business information.

When uncertainty exists, AI must defer to approved documentation or seek direction from the Founder.

---

# Change Management

Canonical documents are updated only through deliberate review and explicit approval by the Founder.

Documentation should accurately reflect approved decisions rather than proposals or ongoing discussions.

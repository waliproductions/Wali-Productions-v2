# Purpose

This document states the Christian foundation of Wali Productions LLC.

It is not included as a marketing slogan. It exists because faith in Jesus Christ is the most important aspect of life and the foundation of the company.

---

# Foundational Belief

Wali Productions LLC is founded on faith in the Lord Jesus Christ.

The company seeks to honor God in its work, relationships, decisions, and public representation.

---

# Christ as Foundation

Jesus Christ is the foundation of Wali Productions LLC.

The company does not place business success, financial gain, public approval, or opportunity above faithfulness to Christ.

---

# Scripture and Business

Wali Productions LLC seeks to conduct business according to biblical principles, including truthfulness, integrity, stewardship, diligence, service, and excellence.

---

# Work as Service to God

Work is not merely economic activity.

Work is an opportunity to serve others, steward God-given abilities, and glorify God through faithful effort.

---

# Integrity Before Gain

Wali Productions LLC should not knowingly pursue work, partnerships, messaging, or opportunities that require compromising biblical conviction.

---

# Public Identity

Wali Productions LLC may publicly identify as a Christian company.

This identity should be communicated with clarity, confidence, professionalism, and sincerity.

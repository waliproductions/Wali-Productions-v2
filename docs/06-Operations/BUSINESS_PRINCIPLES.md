# BUSINESS_PRINCIPLES

## Purpose

This document establishes the operating principles that guide the daily business decisions of Wali Productions LLC.

Every strategic decision, client engagement, partnership, investment, and engineering effort should be evaluated against these principles.

---

# Principle 1

Honor Jesus Christ above every business opportunity.

No contract, partnership, or financial opportunity should require compromising biblical convictions.

---

# Principle 2

Integrity is more valuable than profit.

Short-term financial gain should never come at the expense of honesty, professionalism, or trust.

---

# Principle 3

Serve people with excellence.

Every client deserves professionalism, preparation, respect, and our best effort.

---

# Principle 4

Continually improve.

Learning never stops.

Every project should increase the knowledge, capability, and effectiveness of the company.

---

# Principle 5

Build systems that outlive individuals.

Processes, documentation, automation, and knowledge should be preserved so the organization continues to grow beyond any one person.

---

# Principle 6

Steward resources wisely.

Time, finances, equipment, information, and opportunities are entrusted resources that should be managed responsibly.

---

# Principle 7

Solve real problems.

Technology should simplify work, improve organizations, and create lasting value.

Complexity should never exist simply for its own sake.

---

# Principle 8

Think long-term.

Every decision should strengthen the future of Wali Productions LLC rather than merely solving today's problem.

---

# Principle 9

Remain teachable.

Constructive feedback, continual education, and humility are essential for long-term success.

---

# Principle 10

Leave every client better than when we found them.

Success is measured by the value created, the relationships strengthened, and the trust earned.

# SERVICE_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDE

## Purpose

This document defines the standard used to document every service offered by Wali Productions LLC.

Every service should follow the same structure to ensure consistency across the website, proposals, capability statements, government contracting documents, and future AI-generated content.

---

# Standard Service Template

Every service should document the following:

## Service Name

Official service name.

---

## Description

What the service is.

---

## Business Problem

What problem does this service solve?

---

## Ideal Clients

Who benefits most from this service?

---

## Deliverables

What will the client receive?

---

## Technologies

What technologies are commonly used?

---

## Process

Typical project workflow.

---

## Benefits

What value does this service provide?

---

## Future Expansion

Potential future capabilities.

---

# Documentation Standard

Services should always be:

- Truthful
- Professional
- Technically accurate
- Client focused
- Consistent with the company's mission
- Consistent with the Kingdom Mission
- Consistent with the Statement of Faith

---

# Canonical Rule

This document defines the standard.

Individual services should follow this format unless explicitly approved by the Founder.
